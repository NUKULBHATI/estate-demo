About Sikka Horizons

Home / About
Fig. 01 — Our Story

Eighteen years of building trust, one address at a time.

Sikka Horizons began in 2007 as a two-person advisory helping families navigate Delhi's fast-changing property market. Today we're a full-service brokerage covering residential, commercial and investment real estate across NCR — but the promise hasn't changed: honest advice, verified listings, and one advisor who sees you through the whole journey.

We built our name on the deals other brokers wouldn't take the time to get right — clean title checks, fair pricing, and paperwork that never comes back to haunt you.
